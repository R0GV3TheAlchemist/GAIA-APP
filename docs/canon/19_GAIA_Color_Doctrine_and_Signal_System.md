## Color Use Prohibitions

1. Alert Red may not be used for aesthetic emphasis
2. A critical state may not be displayed in Neutral Grey to reduce alarm
3. Colors may not be used to draw the HP toward a preferred option
4. A LOW CONFIDENCE output may not be displayed with GAIA Green
5. Colors may not simulate warmth, fear, or excitement as social manipulation
