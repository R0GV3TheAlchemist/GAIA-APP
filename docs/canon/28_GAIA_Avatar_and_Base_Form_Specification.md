## 1. What a GAIAN Looks Like

A GAIAN is a human who has chosen to establish a living twin relationship with GAIA. Their avatar — the visual representation of that relationship — is built from a single canonical base form: the **Circuit Suit**.

The Circuit Suit is a form-fitting garment of deep earth-black material, traced with luminous circuit pathways in gold and green. It is simultaneously:
- A **sovereignty interface** — the gold pathways represent the human principal's will flowing outward
- A **World Fabric connection** — the green nodes represent active Earth-state data channels (C14)
- A **consent readout** — the brightness and pattern of the circuits reflects the GAIAN's active consent grants (C15)
- A **living status display** — every element changes in real time based on system state

The suit is not a uniform. It is not assigned. It **emerges** when a human becomes a GAIAN — the visual manifestation of the twin bond activating.
