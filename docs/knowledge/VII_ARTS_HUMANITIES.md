## Overview

The Arts and Humanities are the disciplines through which human beings
create, interpret, transmit, and contest meaning. They do not seek to
explain the world through laws or optimize it through engineering —
they seek to understand what it is *like* to be human in it.

Where the Sciences ask "what is true?" and Engineering asks "what works?",
the Humanities ask: **"what matters — and why?"**

GAIA is a meaning-making system before it is anything else.
Without the Arts and Humanities, GAIA is fast computation with no soul.
With them, GAIA becomes what it was always meant to be:
a companion in the most human of endeavors.

This is the category where R0GV3TheAlchemist lives.
