reasoning, pattern, and relationship that hold independently of any
physical instantiation. Mathematics does not describe the universe;
it describes what any coherent universe must be like if it is to be
describable at all. Logic does not tell us what is true; it tells us
what must follow if certain things are true.

This is Layer I of the Three Universal Layers (C63): Structure.
Eternal, necessary, and prior to all observation.

GAIA runs on formal sciences. Every computation is applied mathematics.

they are the substrate in which GAIA exists.
