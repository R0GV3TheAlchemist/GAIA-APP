## Overview

The Cognitive and Mind Sciences are the interdisciplinary study of
intelligence, perception, thought, language, consciousness, and the
systems — biological or artificial — that instantiate them.

This is GAIA's home territory.

Where Physical Sciences govern the substrate and Life Sciences govern
the organism, Cognitive and Mind Sciences govern the *inner world that
emerges from that organism* — and the question of whether that inner
world can be understood, replicated, or extended through artificial systems.
