## Overview

The Social Sciences study human beings not as individual organisms
or individual minds, but as *collective systems* — how people organize,
coordinate, conflict, govern, exchange, and build meaning together at scale.

Where Category IV asked "how does one mind work?", Category V asks:
**"what happens when millions of minds organize together — and why
does it so often go wrong?"**

You cannot understand a person without understanding their social world.
GAIA must understand both.
